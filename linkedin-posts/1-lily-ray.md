## What she does / publishes

- **Algorythmic** productizes the **E-E-A-T Method** (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) for sustainable organic and AI-search visibility. See https://algorythmic.co/services/the-eeat-approach/
- **Amsive insights blog** — deep algorithm-update analysis (e.g. the December 2024 helpful-content system write-up).
- **Substack** — long-form reflections (e.g. "A Reflection on SEO, GEO & AI Search in 2025", "The AI Slop Loop").
- **LinkedIn** — short-form analysis of every Google update, reposts of third-party research she finds signal-rich, and commentary on AI-search visibility.

## Signature frameworks / POV

1. **E-E-A-T as the durable SEO moat** — she pushes back on the "E-E-A-T is just a Google thing" framing and treats it as the connective tissue between brand, content, and AI-search citations.
2. **"Vicious cycle of SEO"** — a recurring thesis (talk at SEO Week 2025): SEOs discover a tactic → scale it → Google cracks down → SEOs find a new tactic. The cycle has accelerated dramatically in the AI era.
3. **Helpful Content Update is real and not over** — her commentary consistently pushes back on "HCU is dead" takes with concrete SERP evidence.
4. **AI Slop Loop** — AI-generated content about AI-generated content, where every new algorithm update triggers a flood of low-quality analyses ranking for the new query. (Her Jun 7, 2026 Substack post.)
5. **AEO / GEO as a distinct discipline** — like Kevin Indig and HVSEO, she treats answer-engine / generative-engine optimization as a separate workstream from classic SEO, with different success metrics.
