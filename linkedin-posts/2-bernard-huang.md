## Signature frameworks / POV

1. **"The biggest shift in SEO isn't technical. It's cognitive."** ‚Äî Bernard's recurring headline. The shift is from "keywords" to "conversations" ‚Äî the long tail of search is no longer about a thousand keyword variants, it's about a thousand user intents and contexts that AI search must interpret.
2. **"Human Taste" > SEO tactics** ‚Äî in conversation with Ben Brown: "The biggest shift? 'Human Taste' is now more valuable than [technical SEO]." His argument: when AI can do the production, the differentiator is editorial judgment and brand voice.
3. **Live data vs training data** ‚Äî AI search increasingly pulls live data when responding. The split between "what the model memorized" and "what it just looked up" is a key metric. Post: "AI Models' Knowledge Cutoffs Becoming Less Relevant."
4. **AEO/GEO is "SEO continued," not a new discipline** ‚Äî pushes back on the "GEO is brand new" framing. Most of what works is SEO fundamentals applied to AI search surfaces.
5. **AI prompt tracking > keyword tracking** ‚Äî "If SEO = tracking keywords, then AEO = tracking prompts." Implies a new observability stack.
