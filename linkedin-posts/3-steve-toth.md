## Signature frameworks / POV

1. **"AEO is eating SEO"** — Steve's tagline and podcast title. His argument: Answer Engine Optimization isn't a sub-discipline of SEO, it's a new dominant pattern. SEO survives as the foundation, but the strategic center of gravity has moved to AI answers.
2. **PAAs (People Also Ask) as keyword research** — "How to find hidden SEO opportunities with PAAs." PAAs are a free, underused source of intent signals that map directly to AEO prompts.
3. **"Feed AI the facts, or it makes them up"** — title of one of his recent posts. Argument: brands need an explicit, machine-readable "AI info page" (his Notion template) to feed LLMs accurate facts about their product/service. Without it, AI engines hallucinate.
4. **"SEO is dead" tropes are still wrong, but the entry point has moved** — he continues to publish classic SEO tactics (Notebook Agency still does keyword research, audits, etc.) but the front door of strategy is now AEO.
5. **"Google AI Mode just made SEO 10x harder"** — his take on Google AI Mode: it surfaces more conversational queries, makes classic blue-link SEO less reliable, and forces brands to think about prompt visibility, not just rankings.
