## Who she is

Independent SEO consultant based in Switzerland. Specializes in the German-speaking market and works with international brands on SEO and GEO. Educated at Leopold-Franzens-Universität Innsbruck.

- **Current role:** "SEO & GEO Experte | Google hasst dich – aber das kann ich ändern! (P.S. ChatGPT hasst dich auch)" (translation: "Google hates you — but I can fix that! (P.S. ChatGPT hates you too)").
- **Location:** Switzerland (The Circle, Zürich area).
- **Specialty:** German-market SEO, technical SEO, topical authority, GEO (Generative Engine Optimization).
- **Talks:** brightonSEO (multiple appearances), Majestic "SEO in 2026" series, Women in Tech SEO, Search Central Live Zurich, Oncrawl's 12 Days of SEO.

## What she does / publishes

- **Independent SEO consulting** for German-market and international clients.
- **GEO expertise** — among the first German-speaking consultants to productize GEO as a service.
- **Public talks** — frequent speaker at brightonSEO, Majestic events, Women in Tech SEO.
- **Interviews** — Women in Tech SEO, Majestic's "SEO in 2026" series, Oncrawl's 12 Days of SEO.

## Signature frameworks / POV

1. **"Tidy up your online presence for the eyes of AI"** — Dani's most-cited recent talk (Jan 22, 2026, on Majestic's channel). Argument: outdated, inconsistent, or inaccurate information online is no longer harmless — AI engines read it and propagate it. Audit and clean up brand info across the web.
2. **Topical Authority as a step-by-step system** — her Oct 2024 LinkedIn post walked through "Topical Authority: A Step-by-Step Guide." She treats it as a buildable asset, not a vague concept.
3. **"Can you fix my SEO?"** — a real-talk post where she calls out the common client question and reframes SEO as a system, not a one-time fix.
4. **German-market SEO specifics** — much of her work is on the .de / DACH market — regulatory, language, and SERP differences. Few English-language SEOs cover this.
5. **BrightonSEO regular** — appears at the conference multiple times per year. Her talks are a strong signal of her current focus.
